Common questions

Is an account required?
No. Idle Merge Empire can be played without creating an account or signing in.

Where is progress stored?
Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Removing the app may remove locally stored progress.

The game has no sound or vibration.
Open Settings in the game and confirm that Music, Sound, and Haptics are enabled. Also check your device volume and silent-mode settings.
